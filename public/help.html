Channel Curator

Your personal YouTube channel video curator

What is Channel Curator?

Channel Curator is a personal video dashboard that lets you curate content from your favorite YouTube channels in one place. Instead of visiting multiple channels, all their latest videos appear in your personalized feed.

Perfect for staying updated with content from creators you follow without the algorithmic recommendations getting in the way.

How to Use

1

Get a YouTube API Key

Click the Settings button in the top right, enter your YouTube Data API key, and click Apply. Get a free key from Google Cloud Console.

2

Search and Add Channels

Use the search box in the sidebar to find YouTube channels. Click the + button to add them to your list.

3

Configure Content Types

Click the settings icon next to each channel to choose what content to fetch: long videos, Shorts, and community posts.

4

Refresh Your Feed

Click the Refresh button to fetch the latest videos from all your added channels.

5

Watch and Track Progress

Click on any video to play it in the embedded player. Use the checkbox to mark videos as watched.

Tip: Settings

Use Settings to adjust how many videos to fetch per channel and how far back to look. Your settings are saved automatically.

Features

Channel Grouping

Videos are organized by channel so you know where each video comes from.

Watch Tracking

Mark videos as watched with a single click. Status persists across sessions.

Session Persistence

Your channels, videos, and progress are saved automatically.

Embedded Player

Watch videos without leaving the site in the floating player.

Quota & API Usage

This app uses the YouTube Data API v3. Your API key has a daily quota of 10,000 units.

  • Searching for channels: 100 units
  • Fetching videos: 2-3 units per channel
  • Community posts: 1 unit per channel

With optimized fetching, you can refresh your feed hundreds of times per day without hitting the limit.
